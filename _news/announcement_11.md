New position paper available: "Position: Autonomous Scientific Discovery Needs Embodied Experimentation with Learnability" (co-first & co-corresponding author). We argue that autonomous scientific discovery requires embodied experimentation with learnability, and propose an Embodied Science Agent as a proof-of-concept that closes the loop between hypothesis generation, physical experimentation, and iterative learning.
